Boksburg Benoni Hospital - Furniture Supply

The Boksburg Hospital was identified to receive a donation of furniture and other supplies.
The team put together the contribution and presented it to the hospital along with free copies of Rhapsody Of Realities.



Johannesburg Hospital Ward Painting

The ROSA team painted two wards at the Johannesburg Hospital. The ward was in dare need of painting and the objective of the project was to distribute Rhapsody Of Realities as well as ensure that patients had a bright environment in which to stay.

There was an official handover of the newly painted ward to a group of hospital officials consisting of Professors, Doctors and Nurses who expressed their profound gratitude to the ROSA Hospitals Team, Pastor Chris Oyakhilome and Christ Embassy Ministry.
