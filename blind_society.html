Blind Society Visitation


The team members also identified needs within the homes for the blind. Prior to the visit there was a major food donations drive which yielded a tremendous response. With assistance from some church members the team was also able to donate much-needed plastic chair – a need that was identified during the initial meetings with the management of the homes.

More than 40 individuals with varying degrees of blindness were reached at a home for the visually impaired. Ministrations were done in the cafeteria, food items donated, as well as audio CDs of Rhapsody of Rhapsodies.
