Reach Out South Africa

Reach Out South Africa is an annual nationwide campaign designed to touch lives and
build our great nation South Africa with the Messenger Angel, Rhapsody Of Realities.

Realities have been distributed strategically through a colossal community based approach, bringing hope, beauty, color and essence to uncountable number of individuals and groups. As part of the campaign, various community outreach projects that target specific areas of need are held. These include special projects implemented at child-headed homes, Orphanages, abused women shelters, the blind society and many others. With themes such as: “Celebrating 20 Years of Democracy” and "Touching Lives and building our Nation South Africa", nation-building is at the core of the ROSA campaign.

While the campaign encourages South Africans to take pride in their nation and support its leaders in prayer, it is also part of a global campaign that has seen billions of copies of the Number 1 best selling devotional, Rhapsody of Realities distributed in countries around the world. The Rhapsody Of Realities is currently available in 950 languages, including all 11 of South Africa’s official languages.

Since its inception, the campaign has grown from strength to strength, with a huge participation from recipients of the daily devotional and beneficiaries of the community projects. The projects include:

  • Street cleaning and greening projects
  • Food, stationery, and toiletry hampers for indigent and at-risk households
  • Free Dental clinic
  • Refurbishment of public facilities and institutions such as clinics, hospitals, police stations and drug and alcohol abuse centres
  • Distribution of school stationery and school bags to underprivileged schools
  • School library projects
  • Skills development
  • Soup kitchens and feeding scheme

The campaign which usually holds during the Heritage month, in September; always showcases our unity in diversity as a people of color, beauty, strong will power and great heritage; proudly South African.

Aptly known as the Messenger Angel, the Rhapsody Of Realities has made an indelible impact in the lives of millions of South Africa, and continues to do so every day, as countless testimonies has been reported by various individuals and groups.
