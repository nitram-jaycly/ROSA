Projects

Hospital

The team put together the contribution and present it to the hospital along with free copies of Rhapsody Of Realities.

Pregnancy/Abuse

The aim is to raise awareness of the issues of teenage drug abuse and pregnancy, we impart a sense of hope and purpose to the community.

Blind Society

The project aims to distribute copies of Rhapsody Of Realities to the blind, the team members also identify needs within the homes for the blind.

Education

Our team is more concerned with the education of South Africa through distribution of clothes, career guidance, library creation and skills empowerment.

Environmental Care

We contribute to the creation of a sustainable environment through planting trees, food gardening, painting facilities and cleaning streets.

Under Privileged

We are reaching out to all peopla who not enjoying the same standard of living or rights as the majority of people in a society.
